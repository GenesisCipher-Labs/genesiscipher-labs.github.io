# HomeSafe Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 31, 2026**

HomeSafe is a personal navigation tool that helps you compare route options based on safety signals available from public map data. This policy explains what information the app uses and how.

## Data we collect

**Precise location.** When you grant location permission, HomeSafe reads your device's current location to suggest nearby starting points, plot routes, and match routes against safety signals like nearby hospitals, police stations, metro entrances, pharmacies, and 24-hour stores. Your location is used only on your device and is never transmitted to GenesisCipher Labs or any third party.

**Search input.** Addresses and place names you type into the destination picker are sent to Apple's MapKit services to return matching results. This is handled by Apple under [Apple's Maps & Privacy policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/data/en/apple-maps/). HomeSafe does not log, store, or transmit your searches.

**Recent destinations.** The last five destinations you select are stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults so you can re-select them quickly. They never leave the device. Clearing the app or reinstalling removes them.

## Data we do not collect

- We do not run analytics, telemetry, or crash reporting services.
- We do not use advertising identifiers and do not track you across apps or websites.
- We do not operate user accounts. There is no sign-in.
- We do not maintain servers that receive your data.
- We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties.

## Third-party services

HomeSafe uses Apple's MapKit and Core Location to render maps, compute routes, and search for places. Your interactions with these services are governed by Apple's privacy policies.

## Permissions

- **Location (When In Use):** required for route planning. You can revoke this at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services. Without it, the app cannot calculate routes.

## Children

HomeSafe is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

## Changes to this policy

We will update the "Last updated" date above when this policy changes. Material changes will be reflected in a new app release.
